This folder has all of the processed GPS products from the 23 fin whales. Aside from the standard files, there is also a csv, which has
coordinates, mean #of sattelites, residual error, and two date columns, tagtime and datetime. Tagtime is the tag recorded time, use for 
comparing events within the tag (ie, prh). datetime is the corrected "real" time, with the offset approved (seemed to be very minimal, generally +/-
.5 s. Everything is unfiltered, and the excel sheet shows what tags were processed + how many points I got. CSV files are in UTC, not local
